Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions.

How does PathHire find jobs that actually match my skills? +
Our AI parses your resume and builds a deep skill profile — then continuously scans thousands of live job postings and scores each one against your actual experience, seniority, and tech stack. You only see jobs you're genuinely competitive for.
What happens if I'm not happy within the first 3 days? +
No problem at all. Cancel within 3 days of signing up and you'll receive a full 100% refund — no questions asked, no hoops to jump through. We only want you here if it's genuinely working for you.
Does PathHire work for remote-only job seekers? +
Absolutely — it's what we're built for. The entire platform is optimised around remote and hybrid roles. You can filter by timezone, region, or full async-remote and we'll surface only the listings that genuinely match those criteria.
Will PathHire share or sell my resume data? +
Never. Your resume and profile are used solely to power your personal job matches. We don't sell data to recruiters, advertisers, or any third parties. You're a customer, not a product.
How is PathHire different from just searching on LinkedIn or Indeed? +
Those platforms show you every job and let you figure out the fit. PathHire inverts the model — we score and rank opportunities specifically against your profile before you ever see them. Less scrolling, more interviewing.
Can I use PathHire if I'm switching industries or roles? +
Yes. When you upload your resume you can specify your target role even if it differs from your current one. The AI weighs your transferable skills accordingly and surfaces positions where your background gives you a realistic edge — even across industries.
