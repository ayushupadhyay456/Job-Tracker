Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, uploading your resume, or contacting support — including your name, email, resume content, and job preferences. We also automatically collect usage data such as pages visited and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve PathHire — to match your resume against job listings, generate AI scores, send job alerts, and improve our matching accuracy. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. We Never Sell Your Data

Your resume and personal information are yours. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers, data brokers, or recruiters. You are a customer — not a product.

4. Data Storage & Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Your resume is stored securely and only accessible to you and PathHire's AI systems.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your preferences. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, export, or delete your personal data at any time via your Profile settings or by emailing privacy@pathhire.com.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions: privacy@pathhire.com
